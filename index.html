Research Projects

NLP in Social Media

We are interested in stance detection in social media for political affiliation. Stance Detection means automatically detecting whether the author of a piece of text is in favor of the given target, which benefits for information retrieval, text summerization, etc. Since manually labeling is extremely time consuming and tidious, we mainly focus on weakly supervision and semi-supervised learning. Sometimes text may not express explicity an attitude towards a target, and weakly learning may come into noisy labelling issue, in this case, topic-aware methods are needed like using reinforced model to incorporate target-related or cross-target info and then eliminate noisy instances. We are searching the possibilites of transfer learning and knowledge graph for our purpose as well, like feeding semantic-emotion heterogeneous graph constructed from external semantic and emotion lexicons into GraphCNN to learn external connections thus enhancing cross-target stance detection. Besides, we extend the binary classification by Valence Score to see the degree of people's stance and sentiment towards the target. Right now, we are brainstroming a good research direction related and try to come out some publishable research output this coming Spring.

EEG Signal Processing by Deep Learning

EEG signal processing connects heavily with Brain-Computer Interaction. People already have researched a lot in this area like using different fancy and efficient deep learning framwork to detection emotional stages. We pipelined people's models to see if we can enhance the performace. Since the deep leanrning model part is not easy to improve right now, we focus on the signal processing part like implementing and comparing different insightful ways for data preprocessing including shuffling, taking base signal into consideration and projector the signal, etc.
